Good Thoughts

Positivity

Why is it that people are losing faith by the minute? That they don't believe anymore? Yes indeed, there are difficult times. And these difficult times are exactly what challenge you. They challenge you to come out smiling. The truth is, every situation is what the mind makes it to be. That's why they say that the mind is a magnet. Many people interpret this saying differently. Well, yes, all interpretations are in fact correct. Firstly, you attract what you think. If you're always negative, then negativity is what you get. Another thing is, each situation can be taken in a different way. If you make the best of everything, you'll have no reason to frown. Yes it is true, that there is a positive side to everything. So go out there and make the most of what you've got!!!

Friendship

Friendship is the ultimate gift of God. If you've found a friend, you've found a treasure, which is to be cherished forever. Having a true friend is the best experience you'll ever have. They're there with you through the thick and thin. They laugh with you and cry with you. And you know that you can always count on them. They can lighten your mood in a second. You can tell them your deepest secret and can be absolutely sure its safe. They will always love you for you and won't ever judge you. But shouldn't we do our part too? Be as good a friend to them, as they are to us. I am extremely lucky to have found my best friend.... I hope you are too. FRIENDSHIP IS A TRUE DELIGHT!!!

Live Life Your Own Way

It is no secret that everything has an end. That which is born, must one day die, and that which is started must one day be ended. And so is life. Life is a journey, not a destination. It is a journey to be enjoyed. So why do we hold back? Maybe it's because of the rules of the society. Yes, we have rules. To inculcate discipline, and to facilitate the way of life. It is important to respect them. But at the same time, it is also important to make sure that you live life to the fullest. Life is not meant to be spent locked behind bars. Be free and find out what makes you happy. Follow your dreams and don't let anything stop you. Embrace your own self. You don't need to change yourself just to meet the expectations of others. Live your life, your own way!!!
